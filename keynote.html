Keynote Speaker

Dr. Inna Kouper

We are pleased to announce that Dr. Inna Kouper, Luddy School for Informatics, Computing, and Engineering, will be our keynote speaker for the 2022 ILS Doctoral Forum. Dr. Kouper has a Ph.D. in Sociology at the Institute of Sociology in Moscow, Russia (2001) and a Ph.D. in Information Science at Indiana University (2011). Her research interests include the history and sociology of knowledge production and dissemination, data science, data curation and digital preservation, communities of practice, and computer mediated communication and social media.

For more information about Dr. Kouper, see her page on the Luddy website.
